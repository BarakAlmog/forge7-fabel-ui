PLATE VI · THE TERMS FORGED IRON, NOT FINE PRINT

Seven clauses.
No fine print.

  1. I

    You own the machine. Outright. We build it, you keep it. No rent, no ransom, no per-seat tithe.

  2. II

    Fixed price, quoted before we strike. If we misjudged the metal, that is our loss, not yours.

  3. III

    If it breaks, we reforge it. Free. An automaton that stops is our shame, not your problem.

  4. IV

    If the numbers don’t favour you tenfold, we will say so at the audit and send you home with your money.

  5. V

    Your data stays under your roof. The machine works in your house, with your keys, on your metal.

  6. VI

    We will never sell you a dashboard. If you ask nicely: still no. You wanted the hours, not another screen.

  7. VII

    We tend what we forge. Quarterly inspections, oil included, for as long as the machine serves.
